Jeff Mills

TECHNO - from Detroit to the World

Here's a time line of Jeff Mills

  • 1963 - Born in Detroit, USA
  • 1981 - Graduated at Mackenzie High School
  • 1989 - Founded the musical collective UNDERGROUND RESISTANCE among Mad Mike Banks and Robert Hood
  • 1991 - Became resident DJ at TRESOR Club, Berlin
  • 1992 - Founded the record label AXIS
  • 1992 - Released his first solo studio album: WAVEFORM TRANSMISSION VOL. 1
  • 2019 - Released his thirty-eighth solo studio album: MOON - THE AREA OF INFLUENCE
